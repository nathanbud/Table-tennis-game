The Game

- This is the simplest table tennis game. In this game you will play against the computer (red bar). You, on the other hand will be the white bar.

Game Mechanics:

    1. A player will earn 1 point on every opposing players miss.
    2. The first player who earns 11 points will be declared winner.

Controls:

    1. Mouse up to bring the racket up.
    2. Mouse down to bring the racket down.

DEVELOPER NOTES:

This game is far from over. A lot of features and controls are yet to be added.
